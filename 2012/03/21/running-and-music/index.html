Running and music

About running
I started running in May 2007. Today, running is part of my life. So, I decided to share about this tremendous experience. If you want to find out more about running, motivation, diet habits, mental preparation, goals setting and improvement, feel free to join the Anima Sana In Corpore Sano community on Facebook, and don’t forget to like it (if you do, of course !).

About music
Far from being a talented musician, I enjoy playing the piano and blues harmonica to develop my creativity, my dexterity, my breath, my hear and to better understand what makes music so delightful to us. I let you appreciate how I have to improve my musical skills.
